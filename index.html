Roshan Selladurai About Me

Roshan is an Indiana Hoosier with a background in consulting and finance. He is currently working as an equity analyst at Peloton. He is also a growth marketer at New Story Charity, a non-profit startup backed by Y Combinator. Roshan previously held stints in consulting, capital markets, and a human-computer interaction technology startup. In his free time he enjoys playing strategy games with friends, traveling, and reading.
